About

General
Motivation
       Tiltscore was an idea I had while trying to reach my ladder goals near the end of season 7. I had only a couple days left and was about 200-300 lp away from where I wanted to be. After going on a 7 game win streak directly followed by a 7 game loss streak which solidified the impossibility of reaching my desired elo, I thought about what I could do to help myself and others like me from huge loss streaks. Way too often tilted players go into autopilot and continue to play resulting in huge loss streaks. Everyone knows the feeling of thinking that you can turn it around with a win streak. Instead this just baits people into huge drops in ranking because they didn't take a breather.
Idea
       Tiltscore pulls data and statistics from your recent matches and does analytics on four distinct factors that likely affect your level of tilt. After assessing and combining these multiple factors, which are each weighted differently, Tiltscore will give a final score between 0 and 100 that describes how tilted we think you are as well as a minor description to contextualize what that number means. Clearly you can take the score and advice we generate however you like. We also provide a couple more detailed charts to help you understand the breakdown.
Tiltscore Criteria
Loss Percentage
       Simply takes in the raw percent of games won and lost in your last 10 games and puts it into your score.
Loss Streak
       Determines if you are on a loss streak and the length of that loss streak. The amount it affects your rating increases exponentially but caps at 4 losses (subsequent losses won't affect your score any more).
Time Since Loss
       Finds the last loss you had in recent history and affects your score based on the amount of time that has passed since. If the last loss was greater than 6 hours, tiltscore is unaffected.
Counterpart Gold Differential
       Counts the gold differential between you and your opposing laner in the last 10 matches. If your opponent's total gold is above yours by a certain amount, your tiltscore is affected.
